What is ScanFi?

ScanFi helps you understand Finnish products by translating product information to your language. Just scan any product barcode!

How to Use

Open ScanFi Scan the QR code above or open the link on your phone
Choose Your Language Tap the language dropdown in the top-right corner
Start Scanning Tap "Start Scanning" and point your camera at any product barcode
View Product Info See all product details translated to your language!

Add to Home Screen

For quick access, add ScanFi to your phone:

  • iPhone: Tap Share → "Add to Home Screen"
  • Android: Tap menu (⋮) → "Install App" or "Add to Home Screen"
Tip: No app download needed! ScanFi works directly in your browser. Product data comes from the Open Food Facts community database.
