What is ScanFi?

ScanFi helps tourists in Finland understand product information. Simply scan any product barcode and get:

  • Product name translated to your language
  • Allergen warnings (gluten, milk, nuts, etc.)
  • Dietary info (Halal, Kosher, Vegan, Vegetarian)
  • Nutrition facts (calories, fat, sugar, etc.)
  • Ingredients list
  • Nutri-Score and health ratings

How to Use

1
Open ScanFi
Scan the QR code above or visit the URL on your phone
2
Select Your Language
Tap the language dropdown in the top right corner
3
Scan a Product
Tap "Start Scanning" and point your camera at any product barcode
4
View Product Info
See translated product details, allergens, and nutrition facts

Add to Home Screen

For quick access, add ScanFi to your phone's home screen:

iPhone: Tap the Share button → "Add to Home Screen"

Android: Tap the menu (⋮) → "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App"

Tips

  • Hold your phone steady when scanning
  • Make sure the barcode is well-lit
  • You can also type the barcode number manually
  • Product data comes from Open Food Facts community database
  • Always check the physical packaging for allergen info
